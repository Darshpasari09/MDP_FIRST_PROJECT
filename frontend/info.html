How AdaptIQ works

Everything you need to know to get the most out of your sessions.

⌚

Step 1 — Wear Your Wristband

Put on your ESP32-based wristband. It measures your heart rate (BPM) and skin temperature continuously. These signals are sent to AdaptIQ in real time.

💡 No wristband? AdaptIQ defaults to medium difficulty so you can still practice.
🧠

Step 2 — Stress Is Calculated

AdaptIQ uses a simple model to determine your stress level:

😌 Calm BPM < 80 & Temp < 25°C → Hard questions
😐 Moderate Mixed readings → Medium questions
😰 Stressed BPM ≥ 80 & Temp ≥ 25°C → Easy questions
🤖

Step 3 — AI Generates Your Question

A local AI model (running entirely on your computer) generates a unique DSA question for your difficulty level. It picks a different topic each time and avoids repeating previous questions.

⚡ The AI runs locally via Ollama — your data never leaves your machine.
📈

Step 4 — Answer & Track Progress

Answer questions by clicking an option. Green means correct, red means wrong — the correct answer is always highlighted. Your accuracy and Questions Solved count update on the dashboard after every question.

🏆 Complete questions to unlock new nodes on your progress tree. Aim for that 100%!

Common questions

Do I need the wristband to use AdaptIQ?

No. Without the wristband, AdaptIQ defaults to moderate difficulty (medium questions). You can still practice and track your progress normally.

Are my answers saved?

Yes — every time you answer a question, AdaptIQ updates your progress. Your Questions Solved and accuracy persist between sessions.

What topics does AdaptIQ cover?

AdaptIQ covers Data Structures & Algorithms — Arrays, Linked Lists, Trees, Graphs, Dynamic Programming, Sorting, Searching, and more.

What is the progress tree on the dashboard?

It's a visual journey map — each node represents a question you've answered. Completed nodes turn green, the current node pulses, and future nodes are locked until you reach them.
